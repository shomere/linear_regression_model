**Mission:** Predict the closing price of the United States Oil Fund ETF (USO)
using daily macroeconomic and financial market indicators as a tool for
understanding economic patterns and informing investment decisions.

**Dataset:** FINAL_USO.csv — 1,718 daily trading records (Dec 2011 – Dec 2018),
81 features including S&P 500, Dow Jones, Oil Futures, Gold, Silver, Platinum,
Euro/USD exchange rate, and US Dollar Index. Source: Kaggle.
